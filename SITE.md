# Techify — Site Vision & Roadmap

## 1. Vision

Techify is a premium tech e-commerce store specializing in mechanical keyboards, gaming mice, and high-end peripherals. The design system is "Frosted Pearl" — a modern light theme with indigo-violet accents, pearl white backgrounds, frosted glass UI, and Plus Jakarta Sans typography.

## 2. Stitch Project ID

See `stitch.json` in this directory.

## 3. Design System (for Stitch prompts — always include)

- **Background:** `#F8F9FC` Soft Pearl White
- **Section BG:** `#EEF2FF` Light Indigo Tint
- **Cards/Surface:** `#FFFFFF` Pure White with soft shadows
- **Primary Accent:** `#6366F1` Indigo-Violet (CTAs, active, prices)
- **Secondary Accent:** `#A78BFA` Soft Violet (hover, badges)
- **Highlight:** `#E0E7FF` Light Indigo (pills, tags)
- **Border:** `#E5E7EB` Hairline Gray
- **Text:** `#111827` Near-Black headings, `#6B7280` Muted Gray body
- **Glass:** `rgba(255,255,255,0.70)` + `backdrop-filter: blur(16px)`
- **Shadows:** Multi-layer with indigo tint
- **Fonts:** Plus Jakarta Sans (headings 600-800) + Inter (body)
- **Radius:** 16px cards, 999px pills
- **Aesthetic:** Apple/Linear/Vercel inspired. Clean, airy, premium light mode.
